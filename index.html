Your salon may be a success, and clients may be pleased with the services you offer. While this is a great accomplishment, it is not time to rest on your laurels. For any business, it is important to constantly seek out ways to make it better. One way to do this and bring in even more customers is to expand the services you already offer. Here’s a look at the many services salons offer today and how this can impact your business. From the time you were in cosmetology school, you learned to provide basic services such as hair styling, cutting, and coloring hair. There are many other basic services such as conditioning and shampooing hair, giving permanents, and relaxing hair. Some clients may require extra conditioning if their hair is excessively dry or brittle. Still others may want weaves or hair extensions. By providing these basic services, you have just scratched the surface of what can be done. Since many salons are taking on the trappings of a spa where clients can relax, owners are stressing services that help accomplish just that with massage therapy, aromatherapy, and body wraps. In addition, they offer facials and peels. Many offer cosmetic applications as well as pedicures and manicures to give clients total body beauty.
